Privacy Policy for Staðan
Last updated: June 2025

Staðan is a football livescore app for Icelandic football leagues, developed by Birgir Ísar Guðbergsson.

**Data Collection**
Staðan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. We do not have user accounts, registration, or login functionality.

**Local Storage**
The app stores preferences such as favourite teams and display settings locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

**Third-Party Services**
Staðan uses the Sportmonks API to retrieve live football data including scores, fixtures, standings, and lineups. Requests to Sportmonks are real-time data fetches and do not include any personal user information.

**Analytics and Advertising**
Staðan contains no analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

**Children's Privacy**
Staðan does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

**Changes to This Policy**
If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

**Contact**
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at stadanapp@gmail.com
